# Iglesia de San José — versión isométrica (para el barrio)

Para que la iglesia case con los **edificios del barrio** (misma vista ¾ cenital que
`edificios.png`), no la ilustración frontal. Un solo edificio, aislado, para recortar.

**Guardar como:** `iglesia.png` en `assets/`. Yo la recorto y sustituyo la del barrio.

---
### Prompt para Gemini (copiar/pegar)
```
Pixel art building for a 2D top-down ARPG detective video game (Godot), dark noir
urban night, warm window glow, rich detailed pixel shading, in the EXACT same
isometric 3/4 top-down style, scale and outline as a cohesive town-buildings set
(matching brick houses, a town hall and a fire station drawn in the same pack).
Single building: a SMALL STONE PARISH CHURCH — grey stone walls, a sloped dark
tiled roof, a tall bell tower on one side with a visible bronze bell, a round
stained-glass rose window on the front gable, and arched wooden double doors at the
base spilling a soft warm golden light. Isometric 3/4 view (you see the front
façade plus the roof and one side), the whole church fitting in the frame.
Isolated and centered on a plain flat light-gray background color #d7d7d1, NO text,
NO ground, NO grass, NO shadow, NO surrounding wall or trees, clean crisp edges for
cropping.
```

> Consejo: pídela **sola** (no en una lámina con más edificios) para que salga
> grande y con detalle. Si el estilo no casa del todo, añade al prompt: *"same line
> thickness and colour palette as the other isometric town buildings"*.
